Bi-Weekly Seminars Beginning Wednesday September 10, 12:30pm

This biweekly seminar focuses on topics related to transit and cities, and is designed as a salon where transit enthusiasts, geeks and scholars can gather to share knowledge, raise questions, test out ideas, and share ongoing research in a relaxed setting.  You are encouraged to use these biweekly meetings as a forum for an exchange of information regarding planned or ongoing research projects.  Feel free to attend and bounce ideas off the group. Attendees will always have the opportunity to bring topics to the table. 

DUSP's Jim Aloisi and Michael Leong host this informal gathering to spark and  informative and interesting conversations, facilitate a little MIT transit networking, and nurture  new collaborations.
